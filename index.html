How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests or time trials. Instead, we rely on a combination of long-standing brand reputation, aggregated owner feedback from major retailers and forums, and expert reviews from respected wine publications and glassware specialists. We look for patterns in what actually breaks, what feels comfortable in the hand, and which shapes consistently get praised for specific wine styles. Our primary criteria are rim thinness, balance, durability, and value. A glass may be beautiful, but if hundreds of owners report chipped rims or cloudy spots after a few washes, we flag it. We also consider stem strength and whether the glass is truly dishwasher-safe as claimed. The three picks here have stood out across these dimensions for years, not just weeks.
